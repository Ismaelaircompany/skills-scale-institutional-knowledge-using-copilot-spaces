# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

For role-accountability details across ceremonies and project activities, see the [OctoAcme Role Interaction & RACI Matrix](./octoacme-role-interaction-and-raci.md).

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master / Agile Delivery Lead

### Role Summary
The Scrum Master / Agile Delivery Lead strengthens team delivery flow by facilitating ceremonies, removing impediments, and coaching the team on Agile practices.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Identify and remove delivery impediments with Developers and Project Managers
- Coach team members on Agile planning and workflow discipline
- Surface process risks early and support continuous improvement

### Goals
- Improve delivery predictability and team throughput
- Reduce blockers and handoff delays
- Increase team health and sustainable pace

### Typical Communication / Interactions
- Partners with **Project Managers** on delivery cadence and escalation
- Works with **Product Managers** to keep sprint goals aligned to priorities
- Supports **Developers** in resolving blockers and clarifying workflow expectations

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers shape user journeys and interface behavior so teams build solutions that are usable, accessible, and aligned with customer needs.

### Responsibilities
- Create user flows, wireframes, and interaction guidance
- Define usability and accessibility considerations for backlog items
- Validate concepts via research, prototypes, and feedback
- Collaborate with Developers on implementation feasibility

### Goals
- Improve usability and adoption of delivered features
- Reduce rework caused by unclear user experience expectations
- Ensure customer-centered outcomes

### Typical Communication / Interactions
- Partners with **Product Managers** on discovery, user needs, and feature scope
- Aligns with **Developers** on design handoff details and trade-offs
- Syncs with **Project Managers** on design milestones and dependencies

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts translate business problems into clear, testable requirements that teams can plan and deliver with less ambiguity.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements and constraints
- Refine acceptance criteria with Product and delivery leads
- Map dependencies, assumptions, and process impacts
- Support backlog grooming with clear requirement artifacts

### Goals
- Reduce requirement ambiguity and scope churn
- Improve traceability from business need to delivered outcome
- Accelerate planning and acceptance readiness

### Typical Communication / Interactions
- Works with **Product Managers** to clarify business outcomes and priorities
- Collaborates with **Project Managers** to track requirement dependencies and risks
- Supports **Developers** with requirement clarifications during implementation

---

## DevOps / Platform Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps / Platform Engineers enable reliable, secure, and repeatable delivery through CI/CD automation, infrastructure standards, and platform tooling.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain build, test, deployment, and observability pipelines
- Improve environment consistency and release automation
- Partner on reliability, scalability, and security guardrails
- Support incident response and operational readiness

### Goals
- Increase deployment reliability and speed
- Reduce operational risk and manual release steps
- Improve developer productivity through platform enablement

### Typical Communication / Interactions
- Coordinates with **Developers** on build/deploy requirements and operational instrumentation
- Works with **Project Managers** on release readiness and environment dependencies
- Aligns with **Product Managers** on operational constraints affecting delivery scope/timeline

---

## QA Lead / Test Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Leads / Test Engineers define and execute the quality strategy so delivered work meets acceptance criteria and production readiness expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy, coverage priorities, and acceptance validation approach
- Build and execute test plans (manual and automated where appropriate)
- Triage defects and coordinate resolution with delivery teams
- Report quality risks and release readiness status

### Goals
- Prevent critical defects from reaching production
- Improve confidence in release quality
- Shorten feedback loops for quality issues

### Typical Communication / Interactions
- Collaborates with **Developers** on testability, defect triage, and quality fixes
- Aligns with **Product Managers** on acceptance criteria and customer-impact severity
- Partners with **Project Managers** on quality gates and release-go/no-go readiness
